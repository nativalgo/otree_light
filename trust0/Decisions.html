{{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}}’s decision

{{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} moves first.
{{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} may send some, all, or none of the {{C.ENDOWMENT}} tokens to {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}}.
Each token sent to {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} will be tripled.
For example, if {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} sends 2 tokens, {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} receives 6 tokens (2x3=6). If {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} sends 8 tokens, {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} receives 24 tokens (8x3=24).
{{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} will then decide how many tokens to send back to {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} and how many tokens to keep. {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} indicates how many tokens to send to {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} by typing the appropriate amount of tokens on the decision screen.

{{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}}’s decision

{{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} begins with {{C.ENDOWMENT}} tokens. In addition, {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} receives three times the amount sent by {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}}.
{{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} may send back some, all, or none of the tripled amount to {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}}.
({{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} keeps the {{C.ENDOWMENT}} tokens they started with). Any tokens sent back to {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} will not be tripled.
Once {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} has sent some, none, or all of their tokens, {{C.TRUSTEE_ROLE}} will then decide how many tokens to send back to {{C.TRUSTOR_ROLE}} and how many to keep.
